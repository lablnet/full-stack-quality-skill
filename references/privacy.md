# Privacy And Compliance Standards

Use for PII, consent, retention, deletion/export, and regulatory-sensitive projects.

- Collect only data needed for the product purpose.
- Classify sensitive fields and restrict access.
- Avoid PII in logs, analytics, error tracking, URLs, and client-visible payloads.
- Document retention, deletion, export, consent, and audit behavior.
- Encrypt sensitive data in transit and at rest where appropriate.

Review smells: unnecessary PII collection, no deletion path, PII in analytics events, broad admin access, unclear retention, sensitive data in logs.
