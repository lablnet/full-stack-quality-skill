# Accessibility Standards

Use when accessibility needs a focused review beyond general frontend checks.

- Prefer semantic HTML/native controls.
- Ensure keyboard access, visible focus, labels, names, roles, and states.
- Maintain contrast and support zoom/reflow.
- Do not rely on color, hover, or motion alone.
- Forms need labels, hints, validation messages, and error summaries where appropriate.

Review smells: clickable divs, missing labels, focus traps, unlabeled icon buttons, inaccessible modals, low contrast, dynamic content not announced.
